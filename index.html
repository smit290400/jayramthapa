About Jayaram Thapa

JT

Jayaram Thapa

Public Leader & Legal Advisor

A distinguished public servant and legal scholar committed to advancing just governance, institutional excellence, and the rule of law across Nepal.

Jayaram Thapa is a multi-dimensional leader whose career spans the intersecting worlds of law, public administration, and executive education. As a sitting Member of Bagmati Province under the Ministry of Labor, Employment, and Transport, he brings rigorous legal expertise to the formulation of policies that directly shape provincial infrastructure, labor welfare, and economic development.

Concurrently, he leads Liverpool SS/College & University Affiliate Networks as Managing Director & CEO, driving long-term academic vision and institutional expansion. His advisory mandate to Budhanilkantha Municipalities reflects a deep commitment to localized governance and community empowerment. With an LL.M. in International Law and Human Rights and an active Ph.D. in Diplomatic Law, Mr. Thapa anchors every leadership decision in scholarly rigor and international best practice.
